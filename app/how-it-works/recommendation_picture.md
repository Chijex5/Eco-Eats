# How it works page hero image recommendation

## Goal
Show the process and the people behind the system in a human, trustworthy way.

## Subject
A community kitchen scene: a staff member scanning a QR code or handing a meal to a beneficiary.

## Composition
- Wide landscape (16:9 or 3:2).
- Include one clear action (scan, handoff, or pickup) with minimal background clutter.
- Keep space for headline text on one side.

## Color and lighting
- Neutral, soft lighting with warm accents.
- Avoid overly dramatic shadows or harsh fluorescent tones.

## Usage notes
- If used behind text, keep the text area on a calmer part of the image.
- Avoid showing sensitive or vulnerable situations; keep it dignified.

## Accessibility (alt text draft)
"Community kitchen staff member scanning a voucher as a meal is handed over."
