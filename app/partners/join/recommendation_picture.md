# Partners page hero image recommendation

## Goal
Communicate partnership, trust, and professional collaboration.

## Subject
Two people (restaurant owner and NGO representative) shaking hands in a clean kitchen or storefront.

## Composition
- Wide landscape (16:9 or 3:2).
- Focus on the handshake and friendly faces; keep background simple.
- Leave negative space for the headline.

## Color and lighting
- Warm, natural light with neutral tones.
- Avoid busy signage or strong reds that can clash with the UI.

## Usage notes
- If used behind text, apply a gentle overlay for readability.
- Keep faces visible; avoid cropped or faceless shots.

## Accessibility (alt text draft)
"Restaurant owner and community partner shaking hands in a kitchen."
