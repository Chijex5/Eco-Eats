# Donate page hero image recommendation

## Goal
Reinforce trust and show the direct impact of donations.

## Subject
A volunteer handing a meal box to a smiling child or a donor and recipient sharing a moment of gratitude.

## Composition
- Wide landscape (16:9 or 3:2).
- Keep the action toward one side to allow room for the headline.
- Use a clean, uncluttered background (community center, food bank, or open market).

## Color and lighting
- Warm, natural light; balanced skin tones.
- Avoid neon or high-contrast signage that competes with text.

## Usage notes
- If used behind text, apply a soft overlay or gradient for readability.
- Keep faces visible and expressive.

## Accessibility (alt text draft)
"Volunteer handing a meal box to a smiling child at a community center."
